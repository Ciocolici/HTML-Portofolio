The Best Movies According to Cioco

My top 3 movies of all-time

Yes Man

It is a life lesson.
The world is a playground, but it seems we forgot it.
I also really love Jim Carrey. ^^

Lord of the Rings

Although not a film, but a series, it is probably the best trilogy ever made.
I also love fantasy movies a lot.

Guardians of the Galaxy

Also a series of movies instead of one movie, there is something special about it.
From the characters, to the universe, everything is so well done.
There was not a minute of boredom watching any of the GotG movies.
